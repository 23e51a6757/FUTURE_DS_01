## Project Overview
This project analyzes an online retail sales dataset using Power BI to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, customer behavior, and country-wise sales performance. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and actionable business insights for business decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Analyze country-wise sales performance
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Generate business insights and recommendations

## Dataset
- Online Retail Dataset
- Source: Kaggle

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

## Dashboard Features
- Total Revenue KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Total Customers KPI
- Average Order Value KPI
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Revenue by Country
- Country Slicer
- Executive Summary

## Key Business Insights
- Total Revenue: **8.91 Million**
- Total Orders: **19K**
- Total Customers: **4,338**
- United Kingdom generated the highest revenue.
- Paper Craft products were among the best-selling items.
- Revenue gradually declined during 2011.
- Expanding high-performing products into new countries could improve future sales.
